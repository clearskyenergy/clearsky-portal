System & usage

Cycle + calendar aging are modeled additively (industry-standard). Temperature is the dominant driver — 35–50°C roughly doubles loss versus 25°C. Deeper DoD and more cycles/day accelerate cycle aging.

Warranty envelope

From the OEM datasheet — e.g. "6,000 cycles to 70% SoH at 25°C, 80% DoD." Warranty holds only within cycles/day, DoD, and temperature limits; this model flags when your usage exceeds them.

Augmentation

If capacity drops below the maintain-SoH floor, the model schedules an augmentation (adding kWh) and estimates the cost — the line item lenders look for in years 7–10.
