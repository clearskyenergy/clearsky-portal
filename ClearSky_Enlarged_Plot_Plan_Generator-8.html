✏️ 2 · Buildings / OutlinesSTEP 2
+ Add Point

AI Trace (Step 1) finds buildings automatically, and sometimes a best-guess parcel line — verify the parcel against a real survey/plat, since true property lines aren't visible in aerial photos. To trace manually: click + Add Building or + Add Parcel, select it in the list above, then + Add Point and click corners on the photo in order. Move Point drags individual corners; Move Whole Shape drags the entire outline at once — both apply to the active item (highlighted gold). Buildings render with a solid hatch fill; the parcel renders as a boundary line only.

📋 5 · Sheet SetupSTEP 5
Photo Alignment — Nudge to Match Equipment
Auto-captured satellite photos are rarely pixel-perfect — Google's map embed and its geocoding API don't always center identically. Use the arrows above (or type exact values) to slide the photo until it lines up with where equipment was actually placed in SiteMap Designer. This only moves the photo — equipment positions stay fixed.
