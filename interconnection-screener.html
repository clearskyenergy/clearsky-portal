Fast-track eligibility depends on capacity, generator type, POI voltage, and proximity to the substation. The 15% screen compares your capacity (plus existing gen) against the line section's peak load. Non-export storage often bypasses several screens — noted where it applies.

FERC Order 792 governs facilities under FERC jurisdiction (mostly transmission-level). Most distribution interconnections follow the state equivalent (often modeled on FERC or IEEE 1547 / the SGIP), which can differ — this screener uses the FERC baseline. Always confirm against your utility's actual tariff and the AHJ. This is a pre-screen, not a determination.
