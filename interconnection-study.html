Source & system

Source strength drives fault current. If you don't have the utility's short-circuit duty, request it in the pre-application report — it's the single most important study input.

Feeder buses (radial)

Each bus: load kW, DER kW injected here, and the line to its parent (length ft, conductor R and X in ohms/1000 ft). Bus 1 is the POI. Defaults use typical 4/0 AL overhead impedances.
