ComEd Northern Illinois
⚡ BESH Program — Submittal Checklist
Battery Energy Storage Honorarium · DG Smart Inverter Rebate · $250–$300/kWh
Program Summary
• Rebate amount: $250/kWh (C&I) to $300/kWh (residential / small commercial) of nameplate storage capacity • Standalone eligibility: ✅ Standalone BESS qualifies WITHOUT solar — solar pairing requirement removed by IL CEJA (Public Act 102-0662, eff. Sept 2021). Solar is optional. • Customer type: All commercial customers in ComEd service territory (northern Illinois) qualify — businesses, nonprofits, institutions • Rate plan: C&I customers must enroll in BESH rate plan upon rebate acceptance (required condition) • Demand response: C&I customers must participate in ComEd peak load reduction / demand response program (required condition per Rider CS) • Pre-approval: Required BEFORE installation — submit pre-approval application first to reserve rebate amount • Stacking: Stacks with Federal ITC 30% (IRA §48E) + MACRS 5-yr depreciation + potential IL Shines if solar added later • Payment timing: Paid after installation verification and meter confirmation · 60–90 day processing typical • ITC interaction: Utility rebates generally do NOT reduce ITC basis — confirm with tax advisor • Program authority: ComEd Tariff Rider CS · ICC Docket · IL CEJA Public Act 102-0662
