⚡ BESH Program — Submittal Checklist
Battery Energy Storage Honorarium · DG Smart Inverter Rebate · $250–$300/kWh
Program Summary
• Rebate amount: $250/kWh (C&I) to $300/kWh (residential/small commercial) of nameplate storage capacity • Eligibility: ComEd service territory (northern Illinois) · Must be paired with qualifying distributed generation (solar PV) • Customer type: All commercial customers (businesses, nonprofits, institutions) qualify • Rate plan: C&I customers must enroll in BESH rate plan upon rebate acceptance • Demand response: Required participation in ComEd peak load reduction / demand response program (Multi-Year Integrated Grid Plan) • Stacking: Stacks with DG Smart Inverter Rebate ($250/kW solar) + Federal ITC 30% + MACRS depreciation + IL Shines SRECs • Pre-approval: Required BEFORE installation — reserve rebate amount first • Payment timing: Paid after installation verification · 60–90 day processing • ITC interaction: Utility rebates generally do NOT reduce ITC basis
